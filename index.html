Profile

A creative who thinks commercially.

I’m Nick, a commercially minded creative who helps brands grow with clarity, energy and purpose. With 14+ years across premium retail, jewellery, wholesale, eCommerce and international partnerships, I connect strategy with hands-on delivery, bringing together product, people, customer experience and brand storytelling to create meaningful commercial growth.

Across those years I’ve carried £3m+ in revenue accountability and supported 200+ points of sale across the UK and Ireland — but what I care about most is the thinking behind the numbers: why a partnership works, where a brand can stretch, and how to turn a creative idea into something that genuinely sells.

I’m equally at home shaping strategy in a boardroom and standing on a shop floor. I lead teams through change with structure and energy, build trust quickly, and tend to see problems coming before they land on anyone’s desk.

More recently I’ve been using AI to accelerate concept work, strengthen sell-in tools, prototype workflows and sharpen commercial planning, while keeping brand quality and human judgement front and centre.

Based in Manchester, working across the UK, Ireland and beyond — always looking for the next brand worth building.
